# ONBOARDING.md
Welcome to the Car Honda City Website Demonstrating All Cars And Their Prices project. We're excited to have you on board as a contributor. This guide will help you get started with the project and make meaningful contributions.

## What is the project?
The Car Honda City Website Demonstrating All Cars And Their Prices project aims to create a comprehensive website that showcases all Honda City car models, their features, and prices. The website will provide a user-friendly interface for users to explore and compare different car models.

## Where to start
To get started, follow these steps:

1. **Familiarize yourself with the project**: Read the [README.md](README.md) file to understand the project's goals, features, and requirements.
2. **Explore the repository**: Browse through the repository to get an idea of the project's structure and organization.
3. **Check the issues**: Look at the [issues](https://github.com/[username]/Car-Honda-City-Website/issues) to see what tasks are available and what's being worked on.

## Which files to read first
To get a better understanding of the project, start by reading the following files:

* [README.md](README.md): Provides an overview of the project, its goals, and features.
* [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md): Outlines the guidelines for contributing to the project.
* [docs/ARCHITECTURE.md](docs/ARCHITECTURE.md): Describes the project's architecture and technical details.

## How to pick a task
To pick a task, follow these steps:

1. **Check the issues**: Look at the [issues](https://github.com/[username]/Car-Honda-City-Website/issues) and filter by labels (e.g., `bug`, `feature`, `documentation`).
2. **Choose a task that interests you**: Select an issue that aligns with your skills and interests.
3. **Comment on the issue**: Let the maintainers know that you're interested in working on the issue by commenting on it.
4. **Assign the issue to yourself**: If the maintainers agree, assign the issue to yourself to indicate that you're working on it.

## How to ask questions
If you have any questions or need help with a task, don't hesitate to ask. You can:

* **Comment on an issue**: Ask questions related to a specific issue by commenting on it.
* **Open a new issue**: Create a new issue with a question or problem you're facing.
* **Join the discussion**: Participate in discussions on the project's [discussion forum](https://github.com/[username]/Car-Honda-City-Website/discussions).
* **Contact the maintainers**: Reach out to the maintainers directly via email or GitHub messages.

Remember, we're a community-driven project, and we're here to help. Don't be afraid to ask questions or seek help when you need it. Happy contributing!
