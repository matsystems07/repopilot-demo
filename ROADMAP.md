# ROADMAP.md
## Introduction
The "Website Showing All Products Of Unilever In Pakistan" project aims to create a comprehensive online platform showcasing Unilever's diverse range of products available in Pakistan. This roadmap outlines the project's objectives, milestones, and opportunities for contributors.

## Short-term Goals (Next 3 months)
1. **Scaffold Repository**: Set up the project repository with the necessary structure and tools for collaboration.
2. **Generate Docs**: Create initial documentation, including:
	* Project overview
	* Technical requirements
	* Contribution guidelines
	* FAQ
3. **Create GitHub Issues**: Identify and create issues for:
	* Data collection and integration
	* Front-end development
	* Back-end development
	* Testing and quality assurance
4. **Review and Revise Outputs**: Conduct regular review sessions to refine the project's scope, goals, and deliverables.

## Medium-term Goals (Next 6-9 months)
1. **Data Collection and Integration**: Gather and integrate data on Unilever's products in Pakistan, including:
	* Product descriptions
	* Pricing information
	* Images
	* Categories and subcategories
2. **Front-end Development**: Design and develop a user-friendly website with features such as:
	* Product search and filtering
	* Product details pages
	* Category and subcategory pages
3. **Back-end Development**: Develop a robust back-end system for:
	* Data storage and management
	* API integration for data retrieval
	* User authentication and authorization (if necessary)
4. **Testing and Quality Assurance**: Conduct thorough testing and quality assurance to ensure the website is:
	* Functional and stable
	* Secure and compliant with relevant standards
	* User-friendly and accessible

## Long-term Goals (Next 1-2 years)
1. **Launch and Deployment**: Launch the website and deploy it to a production environment.
2. **Maintenance and Updates**: Regularly update the website with new products, features, and content.
3. **Community Engagement**: Foster a community around the project by:
	* Encouraging user feedback and suggestions
	* Creating a forum or discussion board for users
	* Hosting webinars or workshops on related topics
4. **Expansion and Integration**: Explore opportunities to expand the project by:
	* Integrating with other Unilever platforms or services
	* Adding new features or functionalities
	* Collaborating with other organizations or projects

## Contributor Opportunities
We invite contributors to join our project and help us achieve our goals. Opportunities for contribution include:
* **Data collection and integration**: Help us gather and integrate data on Unilever's products in Pakistan.
* **Front-end development**: Contribute to the design and development of the website's front-end.
* **Back-end development**: Help us develop a robust back-end system for data storage and management.
* **Testing and quality assurance**: Assist us in testing and ensuring the website's quality and stability.
* **Documentation and content creation**: Help us create and maintain high-quality documentation and content for the website.
* **Community management**: Join our community management team and help us foster a community around the project.

To get started, please:
1. Fork the repository
2. Read the contribution guidelines
3. Create a GitHub issue or pull request for your contribution
4. Introduce yourself and your contribution in our discussion forum

We look forward to collaborating with you!
